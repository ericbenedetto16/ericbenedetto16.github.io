Projects

Throughout my career as a Full-Stack Developer I have encountered many types of projects. Here are some to take a look at!

NYC Clean Soil Bank

The NYC Clean Soil Bank Program is an initiative run by The Mayor's Office of Environmental Remediation (MOER). I was responsible for designing, and implementing an internal solution using Cloud Technologies and Microservice Architecture to enable MOER to track/manage all Clean Soil Bank Records.

NYC SPEED2.0

SPEED2.0 is The NYC Mayor's Office of Environmental Remediation's (MOER) public Geospatial Application showing Environmental Facts about properties in New York City. I inherited the code-base and began maintaining it, the datasets in Carto, as well as the Google Cloud Hosting Environment.

Learn More

CSI Transit App

The CSI Transit App is a Cross-Platform Mobile Application using React Native and GPS Units with a RaspberryPi I created to alleviate the inconvenience of not being able to track the Ferry Shuttle that is used to transport students to and from Manhattan on my college campus.

Learn More

Angel

Angel was a 3rd Place Winner at the 2019 Steven's Institute HealthTech Hackathon. I was the Lead Backend Developer for this project, where I installed the AlexaSDK on a RaspberryPi and using IBM Cloud AI with Sentiment Analysis, created an at-home medical assistant.
